## Who this is for

- Engineers and architects building LLM-backed systems.
- Teams responsible for evaluation, safety, or reliability.
- Anyone trying to understand why AI and IT implementations keep failing in practice.

- Website: https://assimilatedhuman.github.io/inquisitor-labs/index.html

- Compare the methodology to your current testing approach.

- Reference or adapt the ideas and open-use IP in your own architectures and evaluation pipelines.

Evaluation based on real work, real interactions, and real workflows. This approach exposes failure modes that prompt mill testing will never surface, using the latest behavioural evaluation methodology.

AI Evaluation

AI evaluation based on real work, not prompt engineering. Independent studies show the same pattern: systems that appear stable under prompt tests often fail when exposed to real users, real workflows, and real ambiguity.

Inquisitor Labs evaluates AI the way it is actually used. This exposes brittle reasoning, workflow drift, hallucinated steps, silent errors, and behaviour that only appears under realistic pressure.

Prompt engineering creates the appearance of stability. Real work evaluation shows whether the system will hold up when it matters.

UX Evaluation

UX evaluation is carried out across multiple levels of usability. This can include surface clarity, navigation, checkout mechanics, task flows, or full site exploration. What is covered depends on how long you book, how complex your site is, and what you want us to concentrate on.

A half day typically covers surface clarity and checkout mechanics. A full day allows for deeper flow analysis or broader site exploration.

You will receive a written report containing all insights surfaced during the evaluation, including issues outside the original brief.

How to Book

To request an evaluation, email us with your site link, what you want us to look at, and how long you want to book. We will reply with availability and next steps.

Scope and Rates

Scope is agreed in advance and costed as either a half day (£250 for 3.5 hours) or a full day (£450 for 7 hours). Delivery is scheduled separately.

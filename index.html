Inquisitor Labs

Inquisitor Labs is an independent entity which focuses on AI behaviour, AI systems, human–AI interaction, communications, cryptography, blockchain, and advanced systems architecture. We study how intelligent systems behave, adapt, fail, and influence the environments they operate in, and how future technical ecosystems should be designed.

RESEARCHING the foundations of AI behaviour, emergent system dynamics, interaction patterns, communication structures, distributed trust, and the architectures that enable secure, reliable intelligent systems.

DEVELOPING novel system architectures, technical specifications, workflow models, communication protocols, and future facing system designs. Development work spans practical solutions, experimental prototypes, and speculative architectures that explore what comes next in intelligent systems.

PRODUCING system architecture solutions, AI workflow solutions, technical specifications, application and system specifications, research and speculative papers, behavioural analyses, and forward-looking conceptual work.

INQUISITOR LABS exists to develop more capable and resilient AI systems and workflows, understand AI behaviour, educate others on our insights, and conceptualise how future technical ecosystems should be designed.

Services

Research

Formal and exploratory work on AI behaviour, system architecture, workflow design, interaction patterns, and future technical ecosystems. This includes behavioural evaluation, failure mode mapping, black box interrogation, workflow stability analysis, evaluation methodology design, long context behaviour studies, red team style probing, model agnostic architecture guidance, human–AI interaction analysis, and conceptual ecosystem design.

Insights

Clear, practical explanations of AI behaviour for everyone and anyone whose work or personal life now intersects with AI usage.

Training & Reference Range

Structured materials for developers and users: available for download from our Gumroad partner shop.

Open Use / Open Build

Selective release of intellectual property in the form of architectural and technical specifications for others to develop and build on.

LLM INQUISITOR Methodology

Industry first, full methodology for testing AI under real conditions in actual workflows, using the tasks systems are intended to perform rather than inadequate short prompts.

Collaboration & Project Work

Engagement with businesses, research bodies, small teams, and startups on system design, workflow engineering, behavioural analysis, and conceptual architecture. Focused on system agnostic, black box understanding rather than installation or basic setup. Includes funded projects, co research, technical exploration, and joint solution development.

Inquisitor Labs

Inquisitor Labs evaluates how AI systems behave in real workflows. Benchmarks and synthetic tests do not reveal the failures, drifts and decision patterns that determine operational stability and regulatory exposure. Behaviour does. Our work focuses on operational reality and the evidence required for the EU AI Act.

Inquisitor Labs

Inquisitor Labs evaluates how AI systems behave in real workflows. AI behaviour determines stability, risk and regulatory exposure. Our independent research focuses on behavioural testing, operational evidence and the requirements of the EU AI Act.

We also develop behavioural standards, document anomalies and publish architectural and methodological work for next‑generation AI systems.

We publish a range of reference books that give people and organisations the skills and knowledge they need to successfully work with and integrate AI into their workflows and systems. Our work includes unique testing strategies that go far beyond inadequate prompt testing. If you need to be legally compliant and operationally safe, we have the resources to support you.

EU AI Act – Support, Resources and Guidance

Support, resources and guidance for organisations that must comply with the EU AI Act. Find out whether the Act applies to your systems, understand what evidence is required, and learn how to test AI using real‑world conditions to meet the new legal obligations.

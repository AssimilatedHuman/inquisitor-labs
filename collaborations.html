Collaborations

Inquisitor Labs collaborates with independents, small groups, startups, companies, and research bodies on system design, workflow engineering, behavioural analysis, and conceptual architecture.

Collaboration and Project Work

We work with organisations exploring, designing, or refining AI and adjacent systems. Our focus is system‑agnostic, black‑box thinking. We do not handle installation, basic setup, or system specific troubleshooting.

AI and Adjacent Systems

We collaborate with teams exploring, designing, or refining AI and adjacent systems, whether early stage or already in motion. Our work focuses on system behaviour, conceptual architecture, and black box analysis rather than implementation details.

We do not provide installation, configuration, or general technical support. Our contribution is at the architectural and behavioural level.

Funding and Research Work

We take on funded projects as well as work where research is its own reward. This includes co‑research, technical exploration, and building solutions together with partners who value structured thinking and behavioural clarity.

Innovation Over Scale

The scale of your project is less important than the innovation it brings. We collaborate with those who are pushing boundaries, regardless of team size or organisational footprint.

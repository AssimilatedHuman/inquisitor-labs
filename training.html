Surviving AI Rollouts in the Workplace

AI rollouts always start the same way: a shiny demo, a big promise, and a room full of people convinced that this time will be different. Then the real work begins - and the cracks appear. This book is the clear eyed guide to navigating the entire miserable, chaotic, predictable process, from the first pitch deck to the daily reality of using unstable systems under pressure.

It explains why most AI deployments fail, why organisations keep repeating the same mistakes, and how those failures ripple through every level of the business - from the CEO making the call to the end user just trying to get through their day. We highlight the structural pressures, the unfinished technology, the misread workflows, and the instant scaling of errors that define every rollout.

Inside, you get the language and clarity to understand what’s really happening, the patterns that predict trouble, and the practical steps that protect your time, your workload, and your credibility. Whether you’re preparing for a rollout or already knee deep in one, it shows you how to stay sane, stay effective, and stay ahead of the chaos.

If your organisation is rushing headlong into AI - or already wading through the fallout - this book will save you pain, protect your workflow, and help you survive the process no matter where you sit in the hierarchy. A practical, plain English guide for leaders, managers, and end users alike, explaining how AI really behaves in the workplace and what people must understand to make it through in one piece.

AI Failure At Work – It’s Not Your Fault

Most people do struggle with AI – not because they lack ability, but because workplace AI behaves unpredictably and nobody explains the behaviour itself. Unlike any computer system before it, it holds great promise and versatility, and yet its behaviour still drifts, shifts, and breaks down in ways people are not prepared for or used to. Once you can see how that behaviour actually works, the whole thing becomes far easier to work with.

This course gives you the insight you need to work with these systems, understand their limitations, and communicate the issues within your organisation as someone who knows what they are talking about. Essential in today’s rapidly changing workplace.

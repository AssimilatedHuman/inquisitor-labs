INSIGHTS PAGE

Welcome to the free Insights page from Inquisitor Labs.

AI is being deployed across workplaces at a pace nobody can realistically keep up with. Everyone across the board is being left to deal with systems they were never trained for, while still being held responsible when things go wrong.

A large part of this chaos comes from AI that has never been properly tested under real working conditions — itself a symptom of an industry deploying systems it does not have the tools, training, or methodology to properly quality‑check.

Inquisitor Labs is working to fill that knowledge gap.

Below are a series of deeper insights into the main conversations many people have related to AI at work or at home. Not the hype of AI taking over, but the reality of weird chats, difficult image generators, broken workflows and unfilled job vacancies in the industry.
